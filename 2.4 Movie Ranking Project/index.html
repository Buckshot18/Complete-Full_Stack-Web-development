The best Best Movies According to Jako

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The wall

My favorite movie of all time is The Wall. a 2017 American war thriller film directed by Doug Liman and written by Dwain Worrell. The film follows two American soldiers (Aaron Taylor-Johnson and John Cena) trapped by an Iraqi sniper.

Nobody

Nobody is a 2021 action-thriller film directed by Ilya Naishuller. The film stars Bob Odenkirk as Hutch Mansell, an underestimated family man who becomes a vigilante after a home invasion.

John Wick

John Wick is a 2014 American neo-noir action-thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski. The film stars Keanu Reeves as the titular character, a retired hitman seeking vengeance for the killing of his dog.
